ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Hung. I'm currently a Physics major at Le Hong Phong High School for the Gifted in Vietnam. I have a deep passion for physics—not only in studying the subject, but also in sharing and explaining concepts to fellow enthusiasts.
I’ve had teaching experience at the Christina Noble Children’s Foundation – Sunshine Home for Boys, where I enjoyed helping young learners explore scientific ideas.
I’m also a self-taught learner with a strong interest in creative and technical skills. I began photography, videography, and coding by teaching myself through online resources like YouTube, and have been developing my skills through hands-on practice ever since.
